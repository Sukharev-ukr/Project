Fortuyn

★★★★☆

Grote Markt 23, Grote Market 23, 2011 RC Haarlem

DESCRIPTION:

The food menu at Fortuyn is nothing short of a culinary adventure. Among the highlights are their expertly prepared burgers, available in two delectable varieties: Hereford beef for those who crave the classic, hearty taste, and aubergine option for a delightful vegetarian alternative. What truly sets these burgers apart is the fact that they are cooked to perfection in a Josper oven, infusing them with smoky, grilled goodness.

.

From sandwiches to soups, fresh and vibrant salads, and an assortment of bar bites, there's something to satiate every craving.

.

But it's not just the food and coffee that make Fortuyn special; it's the atmosphere. The open, light-filled space invites you to linger and soak in the surroundings as life in Haarlem unfolds right outside the windows.

.

n summary, Fortuyn is more than just a dining spot; it's a place where you can experience Haarlem's heartbeat. With its excellent coffee, extensive menu, and a front-row seat to city life, it's an ideal destination for those seeking a culinary and cultural journey in one. Whether you're stopping by for a burger or simply enjoying the spectacle of Haarlem, Fortuyn promises an experience that lingers in your memory.

.

I rate that bar as 4/5, thats why it has a fourth place.
