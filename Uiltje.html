Uiltje

★★★★☆

Zijlstraat 18, 2011 TN Haarlem The Netherlands

DESCRIPTION:

The Uiltje brewery is a newcomer to the Netherlands booming crafter brewing scene and opened this friendly bar in 2015. The brewery adds some modern twists to classic methods and creates beers that mix up traditional recipes.

.

All of Uiltjes wares are available from the bar and its taps are changed each season. The interior is deliberately modest and contains a huge taproom with a large red-brick seating area.

.

Now, let's talk about the stars of the show—the beers themselves. Uiltje Bar boasts an awe-inspiring lineup of craft beers, each one a masterpiece in its own right. From hoppy IPAs to rich stouts and everything in between, the variety is a testament to the dedication of the brewers. The staff here are true beer connoisseurs and are more than happy to be your guides through this liquid wonderland.

.

In conclusion, Uiltje Bar in Haarlem is a mecca for craft beer lovers and an absolute must-visit. It's where brews meet brilliance, and it's your second stop on our list, surely a place, where you can gather, share stories, and savor the magic of craft brews.

.

I rate that bar as 4/5, thats why it has a solid second place.
